Story about our hospital

Story about our hospital

And how we get to this point

Welcome to Stellar Multispeciality Hospital in Bangalore, founded by Dr. Seema Sultana and Dr. Shabbeer Ahmed. Initially Dr. Seema Sultana's Women's Clinic, we expanded into a comprehensive hospital offering services in obstetrics, gynecology, radiology, cardiology, pediatrics, and more. Celebrating our first year, we've performed 332 operations, committed to high-quality, affordable healthcare with a patient-first approach and state-of-the-art facilities. Your health is our priority.

Private Care

Introducing Stellar Multispeciality Hospital's Private Care Services, where we prioritize personalized and comprehensive healthcare solutions to meet the unique needs of our patients. With a commitment to excellence and compassion, our private care services offer a range of specialized options tailored to enhance well-being and facilitate recovery. Here's what we offer:

  • Daycare for Elders : Providing a safe and nurturing environment with tailored activities and medical support to enhance well-being.
  • Physiotherapy Services : Comprehensive care is available both in-hospital and at patients' homes, with personalized treatment plans for recovery and mobility improvement.
  • Pulmonology Patient Tracking : Monitoring and managing health records to ensure optimal care and effective management of chronic respiratory conditions, ultimately improving overall respiratory health.
