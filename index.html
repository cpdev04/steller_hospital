About Us

Welcome to Stellar Multispeciality Hospital, a leading healthcare facility in Bangalore committed to delivering top-notch medical services and exceptional patient care. Founded by the esteemed Dr. Seema Sultana and Dr. Shabbeer Ahmed, we stand proudly in Jayanagar, serving the community with excellence since our establishment
We combine advanced infrastructure with the latest medical technology to provide our patients with the best possible care. Our team of highly qualified doctors ensures that each patient receives personalized attention and expert medical treatment.

Services & procedure

We offer specialized services, including obstetrics and gynecology, radiology, surgery, internal medicine, cardiology, pediatrics, and physiotherapy. Our hospital provides comprehensive inpatient services with 24/7 admissions and various surgeries like LSCS, hysterectomy, cholecystectomy, appendectomy, and more.
We distinguish ourselves by providing high-quality, affordable healthcare services. We are committed to making advanced medical care accessible to everyone, ensuring cost-effective treatments without compromising on quality.

Founder

Together, Dr. Seema Sultana and Dr. Shabbeer Ahmed bring their extensive knowledge, skills, and dedication to Stellar Multispeciality Hospital, striving to deliver unparalleled healthcare services to the community.
